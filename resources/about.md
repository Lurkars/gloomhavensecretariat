## Gloomhaven Secretariat: Companion App

**Website:** [gloomhaven-secretariat.de](https://gloomhaven-secretariat.de)
**Source Code:** [github.com/Lurkars/gloomhavensecretariat](https://github.com/Lurkars/gloomhavensecretariat)
**Help / User Guide:** [help.gloomhaven-secretariat.de](https://help.gloomhaven-secretariat.de)

---

### What is it?

Gloomhaven Secretariat (GHS) is a free, open-source **companion app** for the Gloomhaven family of board games. It acts as a digital replacement for most of the physical bookkeeping components, saving time and keeping your table cleaner and your attention on the game.

It runs entirely in your browser with no installation needed, and works on any modern device: desktop, tablet, or phone. It also supports installation (with offline support): as a PWA on any platform, or as a dedicated standalone app on Windows, Mac, Linux, and Android.

---

### Which games does it support?

GHS supports all official editions: **Gloomhaven** (1st and 2nd Edition), **Frosthaven**, **Jaws of the Lion**, **Forgotten Circles**, and **Button & Bugs**, as well as the Mercenary Packs and other official expansions. Several community editions are also supported, including **The Crimson Scales** and **Trail of Ashes**. Solo scenarios are available for every supported edition. Additionally custom content can be loaded via JSON data files.

---

### What does it do?

**During a scenario**, GHS handles all the table bookkeeping so you can focus on playing. At its core it tracks initiative for every figure and keeps them sorted; manages HP and conditions for characters, monsters, summons, and objectives (including automatic condition effects and expiry); draws and manages monster ability cards with automatically calculated values based on monster stats, and attack modifier decks for both monsters and characters; and tracks elements, XP, gold, the loot deck, item states, and battle goals. Beyond that, GHS automates a large number of fiddly rules: monsters are set up automatically per room and section, scenario-specific rules are displayed and often applied without any manual input, and things like long rests, advantage/disadvantage draws, and condition interactions are handled for you. Frosthaven and Gloomhaven 2nd Edition also bring their own special mechanics, and GHS supports those too. Some character abilities are automated as well, so the app handles the bookkeeping even for complex class mechanics. Almost every aspect of a scenario has dedicated tracking and automation behind it.

**Between scenarios**, GHS doubles as a full campaign manager. It tracks character progression (level, gold, resources, items, perks, and masteries), maintains your party and campaign sheets, registers scenario unlocks, and provides a scenario flow chart and a global map. Item management covers unlocks, purchasing, and crafting. For Frosthaven players, the full Outpost is supported: calendar, resources, buildings, Town Guard deck, and Outpost Attacks. Event decks are supported too. Scenario rewards are applied to characters automatically when you finish, and scenario recaps with statistics are available for review.

---

### Highlights for new players

Once you know the rules and what needs tracking, you will immediately recognise how much of the tedious bookkeeping GHS takes off the table. HP dials, condition tokens, modifier decks, element discs, initiative trackers, character sheets, campaign sheets: all of that lives in the app. A full undo/redo history means mistakes are never permanent. Progress is saved locally in your browser with no account required, and you can export save files or connect to the free public server for real-time sync across multiple devices, so every player can follow along on their own phone or tablet. Almost every feature can be toggled on or off in Settings, so you can start with what you need and layer in more as you go. The app is free, open-source, actively maintained, and available in multiple languages.

---

### Getting started in 3 steps

1. Open [gloomhaven-secretariat.de](https://gloomhaven-secretariat.de) in your browser.
2. Select your edition, add your characters, and set your scenario.
3. Hit **Start Round**. GHS handles the rest.

For a full walkthrough, see the [user guide](https://help.gloomhaven-secretariat.de).
